Welcome to Garage@EEE Official Website!

Garage@EEE is a dedicated makerspace in the School of EEE, and we run events, workshops and competitions. Feel free to browse our website!

Events

We have 3 yearly flagships events that are organised by Garage@EEE, namely ENITIO, ESCENDO, as well as IdeasJam. The aim of these events is to foster deeper connections within the students in EEE Community, whilst exposing our student body with industrial ready skillsets.

Workshops

In Garage@EEE, we hold 3 different kinds of workshop, and all of these workshops serve to help the EEE Community to expose themselves to EEE knowledge.

Stay tuned as we will be updating this space for upcoming workshops that are happening!

About

Garage@EEE is an informal Makerspace run by students for students. It is a learning platform that aims to cultivate entrepreneurial and innovative spirit. We hope to bridge the gap between classroom and experiential learning through ownership empowerment.
